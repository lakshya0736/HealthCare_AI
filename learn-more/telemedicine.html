📱 Telemedicine Services

Consult doctors remotely with AI-powered guidance, saving time and cost for rural families.

🌐 Language: English | हिन्दी

Many rural families travel long distances for basic medical advice. Telemedicine allows doctors to consult patients over phone or video. With AI support, initial triage and guidance become even more effective, reducing unnecessary hospital visits.

Our Telemedicine Support Includes

  • Video/Audio Consults: Talk to certified doctors without leaving home.
  • AI Symptom Checker: Get instant guidance before the consultation.
  • Specialist Access: Connect with pediatricians, gynecologists, and more.
  • Follow-up Care: Doctors provide digital prescriptions and follow-up reminders.
  • Offline Support: SMS/IVR based advice for low internet areas.

How Our AI Chatbot Helps

  • 🧑‍⚕️ Collects symptoms before doctor call to save consultation time.
  • 📖 Explains doctor’s advice in simple local language.
  • 📱 Sends reminders for medicine and next consultation.
  • 🌍 Connects users with verified telemedicine platforms.

✅ Benefits of Telemedicine

💰 Saves Money

No travel expenses and lower consultation charges.

⏰ Saves Time

Immediate consultations without waiting in hospital queues.

🏡 Comfort

Get medical advice from home, especially useful for elderly and children.

🌍 Access

Connects remote villages to urban doctors and specialists.
